Battlegrounds Mobile India

The Indian version of PUBG Mobile, exclusively for players in India.It is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by Krafton.The game was initially released on 2 July 2021 for Android devices and on 18 August 2021 for iOS devices. As of July 2022, BGMI surpassed 150 million downloads on Google Play store The game was banned in India on July 28, 2022, by the Indian government.According to a statement from Krafton, the ban was removed on May 26, 2023.
